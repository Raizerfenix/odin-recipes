Pizza

Description

Simple and delicious neapolitan style pizza, easy to make, and traditional as if made in Naples itself.

This recipe can be done with items found in your everyday grocery store, alongside your average joe's kitchen utensils.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and the salt.
  2. Add in the water.
  3. Add in the yeast.
  4. Knit the dough.
  5. Cover the dough and let it proof for up to 24 hours.
  6. Separate the dough in four parts.
  7. Open up the dough.
  8. Add tomatoe sauce.
  9. Add the Mozzarella Cheese.
  10. Add the fresh Basil.
  11. Add a drizzle of Olive Oil.
  12. Put it in the oven and bake it at 250Â°C for 30min.
