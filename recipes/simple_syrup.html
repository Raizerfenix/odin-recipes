Simple Syrup

Description

Simple and delicious syrup, easy to make, and super efficient on cocktail recipes.

This recipe can be done with items found in your everyday grocery store, alongside your average joe's kitchen utensils.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Add in the water to the pan.
  2. Add in two parts of sugar per part of water.
  3. Keep stirring until all sugar disolves.
  4. Pour it into a bottle for storage.
